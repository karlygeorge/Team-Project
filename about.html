Hello, welcome to Javawocky coffee bar! We are a local independent cafe specialising in coffee and wine. We are family owned and try to source as much as we can from local producers. We sell an exciting range of delicious wines, not to mention some of coffee beans from one of the best roasters in the country. If you’re looking for a decent bottle for supper, a special present or just some coffee to have with friends please do drop by. And if you can’t get here in person, we’re pleased to be able to offer a hand picked selection of our top 100 wines online. With a case discount and very reasonable deliver charge, what’s not to like? We look forward to welcoming you to our place soon! Love, Team Javawocky. x
